    EyeSighth


    EyeSight is a mobile application that helps visually impaired people to be aware of their surroundings. EyeSight uses a rear camera and a speaker or an earphone, processes each frame that is recorded from the rear camera and analyzes the objects in the frame. After analyzing the objects in the frame, it gives constant stream of information to the user through speaker or earphone.
